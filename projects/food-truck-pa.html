About

The Food Truck Chef Playable Ad was an interactive advertisement built for the publisher "Tilting Point." We got to be side by side with Tilting Point, using their feedback to get this one perfect.

This is only one of the many interactive advertisements we've done with Tilting Point, and has been a hit with publishers & studios alike. The nature of the advertisement brings a new level of engagement for the viewers, and is much more exciting than the boring interstitial.

We took advantage of our HTML5 skills, and created everything in pure Javascript to ensure compatability with all ad networks. The ad was entirely under 1MB, with no external references. That includes all images and code.

Design

Design was done by the amazing people at Nukebox. All graphics, animations, and effects were created by them and we adapted them to fit the environment and size limits.

Development

Development was done with HTML5. The game is built on an HTML5 Canvas element, with Javascript building a majority of the functionality.

Platforms

The game was developed for mobile (iOS & Android). Since it's built in HTML5, it's runnable in any web environment that supports Canvas. The game adapts to all screen sizes.

Distribution

The ad was distributed through AppLovin, InMobi, and Google Adwords ad networks. Each network needed its specific implementation, and we took care of that.

Design

While the team at Nukebox Studios took on all the graphical work on this project, we still had a very fundamental part in the design. Being able to bring these graphics to their lowest possible size, while maintaining as much beauty as possible, was a task on its own.
